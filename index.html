First post

It is 25th of July, 2013. 10pm, Sydney time.

First posts are hard.

It's true. The number of times I've sat at a computer, staring at the blinking cursor, willing myself, to just write something so I could be done with it and move on, are simply dizzying.

This post itself has been too long in the making. I'm not proud to say that there have been times I simply gave up and walked away.

But I digress, if a first post is simply about the fact of it being a first post, then I've failed. No one wants to descend into that self-referential mess.

So why this? Why now?
